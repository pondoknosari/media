Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Media ("we", "us"), operated at media-lnwz.onrender.com, collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our social media content management service ("the Service").

1. Information we collect

  • Account information: your name, email address, and password (stored securely, hashed).
  • Connected platform data: when you connect a social account (TikTok, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube), we receive access tokens and basic profile information needed to publish and manage content on your behalf, limited to the permissions you grant.
  • Content you create: posts, media files, captions, and scheduling data you submit through the Service.
  • Usage data: basic technical logs (e.g. IP address, browser type) used to operate and secure the Service.

2. How we use your information

We use your information only to provide the Service: to authenticate you, to schedule and publish content to the platforms you connect, to display analytics, and to maintain the security and reliability of the Service. We do not sell your personal data.

3. Data from TikTok and other platforms

Data obtained through platform APIs (such as TikTok's Login Kit and Content Posting API) is used solely to perform the actions you request within the Service. We handle this data in accordance with each platform's developer terms and policies. We do not share platform data with third parties except as required to operate the Service or comply with law.

4. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can disconnect any social account at any time, which revokes our stored access tokens for that account. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

5. Security

We use industry-standard measures, including encryption of access tokens and secure transport (HTTPS), to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your data.

6. Your rights

You may access, correct, or delete your personal information, and revoke platform connections, at any time from within the Service or by contacting us.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

8. Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact us at admin@media-lnwz.onrender.com.
